Bitcoin Stability

This is the re-implementation of Azop's charts showing the stability of the market price of Bitcoin.

If you want to discuss the project, start a chat at the project's issue tracker.

The source for this site can be found at Github.


Currently, the Bitcoin price stability at time $T$ is calculated as the inverse-standard-deviation of the last $N$ days: $$Stability(T) = {\sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{i=T-N}^{T} (x_i - \overline{x})^2}}^{-1}$$ where $N=6$ days
